ROADMAP FOR CSE SECOND YEAR





Whichever is your 2nd year, that is very productive in terms of learning. Because after that, you think mostly about placements and in the 1st year, your time goes in exploration.

If you have not done DSA yet then this is the right time for you to start that.In 3rd sem you will have DSA as your academic subject

Give your 3rd semester to data structure and little bit of competitive programming.

And someone who has not learnt it, he must leave everything else and must start DSA right from the basics.

Before starting DSA,get hold of one language, learn its functions and loops thoroughly ASAP.

Then solve problems in data structure algorithms like arrays, strings, Linked List, Recursion, Dynamic Programming. Solve problems around all these concepts, solve 250-300 questions solve 20-30 questions from each topic and make your base.

In your winter/summer vacation explore new skills.Also find how to make resume and of what content it consists of?

Building a strong RESUME is very important.Apart from DSA , we need to add some projects. There aren't good projects in data structure algorithms.

So we must focus on some projects like it can be in web development, in app development, it can be in machine learning.Explore your interests in period of vacation

So the very first thing is you need to do as a second year student is to Master the DSAand get hold of any skill.

And the next thing is to create your account on Linkedin and Github and maintain good profiles by uploading your projects

Third thing is to focus on your core and academic subjects like Operating System(OS),DSA and Design and analysis of algorithms(ADA).

In this year it is also mandatory for students to do a Vocational Training in their field of interests and to make any basic project regarding what they have learnt

College provides students a particular duration(45days) to complete their training and creation of project which they are required to show at the time of 5th Sem

Do focus on your training and project ideas!!!

Apart from these things,participate in clubs as a member/lead or in co-curricular activities to explore as much as possible
